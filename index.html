How to start

Around May 1997, Sifat Ibn Siraj, the visionary of our organization, first talked about a focused initiative in service mentality with a few people he knew. None of the others he talked to, including himself, had yet left puberty. Those with whom he finally had a mental union that day were-

  • Shyam Bin Azad
  • Habibur Rahman Nayan
  • Rafiqul Islam Badal
  • Sawgat Ibn Siraj
  • Asif Iqbal Chowdhury and
  • Nazmul Alam Jinjir.

- The organization has not been formalized yet. These few discuss and exchange some helpful ideas. It was not a formal journey either. On July 13, these 7 people expressed their wishes to some of their peers. Everyone agreed to do something. Perhaps that was the first informal meeting of the organization.

25 July 1997 English; 10 Shravan 1404 Bangla, 955/A, Khilgaon Chowrasta address of the entrepreneur's residence with a total of 210/ with a subscription of 30/ formal journey of emotion. Government Registration: In the middle of 1998, the then General Secretary and Deputy General Secretary went for Government Registration at the then Chamelibagh Social Welfare Office. Apply for name clearance knowing the rules and regulations. Due to special reasons (?) they did not have any dealings with that official at that time. And because of that, there is no time to lose the application form, lost in the depth of the file.

At the end of 1999, the application was again renewed, that time with the same problem. Frustrated with two or two unexpected problems, the executive council at that time completely abandoned the idea of official registration. But despite the great reluctance to secure legal facilities, social security and above all to gain people's trust, the current general secretary ventured to register at the end of 2002. The first step of registration is to apply for name clearance. There, the application for the license named 'ABEG' was rejected two or two times. Since the new social welfare rule is to apply against names with full meaning, we seek clearance for a name that has the same meaning as 'ABEG'. At the same time, knowing that the name will change, we come to unity in the practice of English, the internationally understood language in naming.
